Hi. My name is Alan and I'm a Full Stack web developer. What do I mean by that. To me, a Full Stack Developer is someone with familiarity in each layer, if not mastery in many and a genuine interest in all software technology. I have spent the last three years learning and coding for my job as a Technology Consultant in one of the most elite school districts in the country.

In that capacity I have set up and maintained a LAMP server for the school to allow Teachers and Students to learn Web Development in the classroom. Secondly, developed an Inventory tracking system for the Highschool and also a Trouble Ticketing application for Teachers and Staff to more easily contact us with problems and be able to track their status. Also developed a Test Quiz app for Teachers to quickly create quizzes. These quizzes can then be administered online and stored in MySQL database. I have also assisted Staff with training on different Web technologies in the classroom.

I count in my skill set proficiency in HTML5, CSS, Javascript and jQuery. My back-end skills include PHP and MySQL. I have also attained my Cisco certificate as a Network Associate. This is gives me the ability to have the knowledge to understand the whole picture. Good developers who are familiar with the entire stack know how to make life easier for those around them. This involves understanding what can break and why, taking no resource for granted.
